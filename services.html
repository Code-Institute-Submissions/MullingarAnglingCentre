Services

Michael Flanagan

Pike and Trout angling guide

Midlands Angling provide a top class Guiding service covering many of the top coarse and game waters across the Midlands. Visiting fly anglers have landed some fine trout while out with Midland Angling Guides, pike anglers also recorded some net bursting catches including many pike over 20lbs. Some of our top waters include Lough Ree, Ennell, Owel, Lene, Mount Dalton, and Glore Lake just to mention a few. On these splendid lakes we provide full Gillie services. Also the River anglers are well catered for, as we spend many days out on the River Inny, River Suck, and Brosna system.

Alan Broderick, Irish Angling Safaris

Pike fishing guide

Irish Angling Safaris (Ireland Pike) was established by Alan Broderick to promote and market pike fishing holidays on the unspoiled, pristine waters of County Westmeath in the Irish Midlands. Today Alan invites us to visit his angling facility on the shores of Lough Derravaragh, Co. Westmeath and experience dynamic pike fishing. Facilities include fully equped custom built American fishing boats and up todate information on fishing hotspots and the hottest tactics and tackle of the day.
